About Me

Hello! My name is Daniel Douglas.
I'm a Software Engineer from Boston, MA.

In 2024, I decided to turn my long-time passion for technology into a full-fledged career in software engineering. After building my first PC in 2020, I developed a deep interest in coding and problem-solving, leading me to join Resilient Coders. My career journey has been defined by dedication, growth, and leadership having worked my way up from a delivery driver to dispatch lead and later managing warehouse operations. I am eager to apply my problem-solving abilities, leadership experience, and passion for technology to a dynamic and innovative team. Outside of career focus, I enjoy going to the gym, coding, and playing video games.
